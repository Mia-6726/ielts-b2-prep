Study Tips for IELTS B2

  • Listening: Practice with podcasts or English shows to improve comprehension.
  • Reading: Read articles from BBC or The Guardian to enhance vocabulary and speed.
  • Writing: Write essays daily and seek feedback to improve structure and grammar.
  • Speaking: Practice with a partner or record yourself to gain fluency and confidence.
  • Time Management: Simulate test conditions to get used to the time limits.
